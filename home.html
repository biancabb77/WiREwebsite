Women in Robotics and Engineering

Our Sponsor: The Philadelphia Robotics Coalition

Our Goal

WiRE is a 12-week afterschool program for girls in Philadelphia to gain experience with engineering and robotics through hands-on experiments. Specifically, we focus on:

  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Software Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Agricultural Engineering
  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
